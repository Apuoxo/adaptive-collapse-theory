## In simple words

Imagine an old smartphone. At home it works perfectly. But one day you take it outside in the cold — and it suddenly dies. Why? Because its battery (the hidden stability resource) was almost exhausted, even though the phone looked healthy.

This is how any complex system works: a living organism, a bridge, an AI model, a language, or a company. It spends its hidden internal resource just to remain ordered. When the resource runs out — collapse is sudden, inevitable, and **calculable**.

This repository contains the full mathematical formulation of **Adaptive Collapse Theory**.

---

## Key idea

> Any ordered system has a hidden stability margin **L**.  
> It decays over time: **L² = L₀² — γ·t**  
> When L → 0, collapse occurs.

The theory unifies entropy, aging, and system failure into one deterministic framework.
